Lemon Posset Recipe

A delicious English dessert that is made possible with only 3 (yes three) ingredients...

Author: Zeynel Acar

Prep Time: 5-10 minutes Cooking Time: 10 minutes Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 lemons
  • 600 ml cream
  • 150 grams of sugar

Directions

  1. Zest the lemons. After zesting squeeze the zested and set asied the lemon juice and lemon zest.
  2. Pour the cream into a pot and add the sugar. Mix them over medium temperature until sugar is dissolved and starts bubbling.
  3. Remove pan from heat and stir lemon juice and zest.
  4. Pour even amounts into four ramekins. Refrigerate until set.
